## Overview

**MPL Box Fit** is a boxing-fitness ("boxfit") gym. The logo reads "MPL" over
"BOXFIT" inside a hexagonal crest with crossed boxing gloves, in red / white /
black.

- **Type:** Boxing / fitness training gym, with strength & conditioning kit
  alongside the boxing equipment.
- **Location:** Haslingden, Rossendale, Lancashire.
- **Venue:** Based inside the Monkey Playland premises.
- **Origin story:** Mohammed Sadiq, owner of Monkey Playland, trained with
  Umair. They decided to work together and create the MPL Box Fit studio inside
  Monkey Playland.
- **Coach:** Umair teaches beginner boxers the correct fundamentals while
  helping people get fitter and gain confidence.
- **Scale (inferred):** A small, private / boutique studio — a single long,
  narrow corridor-style training room rather than a large open floor.
- **Audience (observed):** Adults and children — at least one photo shows a
  junior boxing session, with an adult coach present.
