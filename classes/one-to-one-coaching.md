      ## How It Works

      A one-to-one is just you and a coach, so the session moves at your
      pace and focuses on whatever you need: stance, jab, hooks, defence,
      pad work, bag drills, fitness or getting ready to spar. Beginners get
      the basics without an audience, and more experienced boxers get
      someone holding pads and picking apart the details.

      Private group training is available too, so two or three people can
